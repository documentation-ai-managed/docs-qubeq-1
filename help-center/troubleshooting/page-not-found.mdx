---
title: Page Not Found Error
description: How to resolve 'Page Not Found' errors in qubeq.
---

## Issue

- You see a 404 or "Page Not Found" message in qubeq
- Your document link doesn't open

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the link">
    Make sure the URL is correct and uses the right project and document IDs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh or login">
    Try refreshing the page or logging in again if your session expired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look in the project list">
    Open your project dashboard and check if the document still exists; it may have been moved or deleted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
