## Getting Started

To create your first documentation project in qubeq:

1. Log in to your qubeq account.
2. Click the **New Project** button on the dashboard.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click **Create** to initialize your project space.
5. Start adding documents and organizing them into folders.

## Tip

You can invite team members anytime using the **Invite** button in your project settings.
