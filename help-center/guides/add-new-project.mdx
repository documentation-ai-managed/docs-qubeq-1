---
title: Creating a New Project
description: Quick guide to adding a new documentation project in qubeq.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open dashboard">
    Log in to qubeq and go to your main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Project">
    Find and click the **New Project** button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter your project name (required) and description (optional).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **Create**. Your new project appears in the dashboard. Now you can start adding documents or folders.
  </Step>
</Steps>
